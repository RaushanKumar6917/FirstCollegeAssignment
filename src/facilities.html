Hostel & Mesh

Residential facilities are available with providing well-furnished hostel for both boys and girls located within stone throw of the College with round the clock service for the safety and security of students. Presently, many girls and boys are accommodated in College hostels. The hostels are guarded round the clock by able private security staff. Patrolling has also been intensified by state security personnel during night.


There are also separate wardens for boys and girls hostel in order to maintain discipline and decorum on the campus. Each hostel provides a mess facility for breakfast, lunch and dinner under the safe and hygiene environment.


Uninterrupted safe drinking water and electricity supply are also provided. Hostels have separate wifi connection for internet facilities to students. Newspapers and magazines are available in the common rooms. Cleanliness and hygiene are of paramount importance and maintained to the hilt. Besides we also come up with first-aid treatment to the students and in case of emergency we have facility to call in doctors or ambulance immediately.

WIFI

Today's world can't be imagined without internet. Wi Fi access to students, faculties and staff is available on the campus and hostels. It enables students to carry out their projects and search for relevant information. The facility is regularly checked and updated as per requirement. The internet access is provided by BSNL(1 Gbps) , PGCIL (100 Mbps) , RailTel(10Mbps).
