OVERVIEW

School of Management and Planning & Advanced Computer Training (VIVEKANAND)school, affiliated to CBSE, Patna was founded by the group leaders in 2014 and has been carving out a niche for itself as the leading institution in Bihar in terms of imparting quality education to undergraduate students


Vivekanand School aims at producing industry-ready professional, focusing on providing theoretical knowledge to students as well as training them for jobs that are based on practical activities. The institution continually is evolving to meet its goal in ever changing business environment.


Besides, offering the best in academics IMPACT College encourages an overall development of the students through various extracurricular activities conducted and taught by talented, experienced and capable faculty members. Apart from academic pursuit, College leaves no stones unturned in instilling importance of personal qualities, social values and principles of professional excellence into the minds of students.


The vision of Vivekanand School, offering undergraduate courses, is to emerge as Centre of Excellence in all facets of Management, Information Technology Education Commerce and Communication, rooted in Indian ethos and societal values.


Believing that development of quality education system largely lies on dedicated members of teachers, staff, quality infrastructure, supportive administration, large pool of learning resources, hardworking and brilliant students, Vivekanand School offers intellectual depth, abundant resources, and individual attention to students. To its alumni, College provides a lifelong connection to excellence. To the world, it has a commitment to ethical and responsible leadership.


Vivekanand School, since its inception, has been providing scholarship to meritorious and deprived students as financial help for their bright and rewarding future and is committed to stopping migration to other states from Bihar by creating state-of-the-art teaching methods, facilities, classrooms, library and computer lab along the lines of professional Colleges in the country.
